Official Recruitment Website Redesign Project @ E.SUN FHC

Overview

The purpose of the Official Recruitment Website Redesign Project is to create a visually appealing and user-friendly website that will effectively communicate our company's brand and culture, and attract top talent to apply for job opportunities. The redesign project will focus on enhancing the user experience and streamlining the application process, making it easy for candidates to learn about our company, search for job openings, and submit their applications. The goal is to increase the volume and quality of job applications, and ultimately, to find the best fit for our company and its employees.

My Role

This project was designed and developed from Nov 2020 to Feb 2021. I worked on this project as a Product Designer and was responsible for UX design (including delivered solutions and created prototype), visual design, and front-end development.

Platforms & Screen Sizes

Web iPhone and iPadAndroid mobile and tablets

The Problem

The HR team reported high bounce rates and low job application rates on the company's old recruitment website. Upon analyzing the report, I identified three key areas for improvement:

  • The action buttons were not prominently displayed and the page contained an overwhelming amount of information, making it difficult for users to navigate and proceed with the application process.
  • The website's structure was confusing and the user flow was unclear, adding to the frustration of potential job applicants.
  • The outdated design of the website did not align with the company's core value of embracing digital transformation.

There were complex hierarchy and too much information in the same tab, making it difficult to find action button.

Competitor Analysis

I conducted competitive analysis to understand the best practices in candidate onboarding processes. Through research of other job sites and recruitment websites of competing banks in Taiwan, I discovered several key insights:

  • It's essential for candidates to have a clear understanding of a company's core values and hiring process.
  • The onboarding process should be quick and straightforward.

Design solutions

UX Design

I collaborated with the HR team to reconstruct the user flow and create a more intuitive and streamlined experience for job applicants. We consolidated job category information, company introduction, interview process and Q&A into a unified, single page. By redesigning the user flow, we were able to reduce the number of steps required to apply for a job from several steps to just two. This not only makes the process faster and more convenient for users, but also helps to increase the number of completed applications and improve the overall candidate experience.

UI Design

The updated website will feature a modern and innovative UI design that incorporates illustrations and motion to create a friendly, youthful, and vibrant atmosphere. The use of illustrations and motion will enhance the user experience and make the website feel engaging and interactive, encouraging users to explore the site and learn more about the company and its job opportunities.

The updated website effectively represents the company's brand and make the process of finding and applying for job opportunities as simple and efficient as possible.

Outcome

The outcome of the Official Recruitment Website Redesign Project will be a high-performing, visually appealing, and user-friendly recruitment website that meets the needs of both the company and its potential candidates. The project will result in a 40% increase in the number of candidates applying for job openings, as the new website design will make it easier for potential candidates to find job opportunities and apply for them.
